This is the Shipping Blockchain's home page.

All Participants must be created in order to create Nomination.

After a Nomination is created, it needs to be verified by the Captain in order to do Loading, Discharging and Sending Email

Nomination - is where Voyage Managers enter the data from the text field. It is then email to their superior for validation purposes.

Discharge - is where it keeps the reference ID from Nomination and records the timestamp of the Hose Connection and Hose Disconnection where the user can also Generate/Send Invoices to the according Participants.

Loading - combines both the Nomination's and Discharge's numerical data and does function calculations which adds the additional pay to the grand total.

Invoice - prints out all the information gathered from Nomination, Discharge, and Loading and gets emailed to the according participants.


1.) Creating Participants

  • Under the Participants tab, create Terminal, Voyage Manager, Shipping Company, Captain, Charterer

2.) Creating a Nomination form page

  • Fill out all empty fields and click Create Nomination

3.) Captain's Verification

4.) Timestamp for NORTendered and Documents on Board in Loading

5.) Timestamp for connect and disconnect hoses in Discharge

6.) Sending Invoice, Freight Calculation via email to associated emails
